Best Casual Dining in Branford, CT: What Makes Longley's Different

By Mike Longley · April 6, 2026

Branford has no shortage of places to eat, but when it comes to casual dining Branford CT locals actually rave about, Longley's stands apart. Since opening in 2024, we've built a reputation on three things: homemade food, great drinks, and service that makes you feel at home.

Homemade Everything

We make everything from scratch. Daily soups, hand-formed burgers, hand-breaded appetizers, and rotating specials — including favorites like our prime rib French onion soup and steak Oscar topped with crab and asparagus.

A Full Bar with Character

Craft beers, wine, and handcrafted cocktails, poured at a bar that's the heart of the room. Swing by for happy hour every Friday from 3 to 6 PM and see why the regulars never miss it.

Open Late, Unlike Everywhere Else

Our kitchen runs until 1 AM Tuesday–Thursday and 2 AM Friday–Saturday — the full menu, not a snack list. If you're on the late shift, we've got you.

Events, Catering, and Private Parties

We host private parties and corporate gatherings, cater across the Branford shoreline with our full catering menu, and bring in live music every weekend.
