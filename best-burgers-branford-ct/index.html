Best Burgers in Branford, CT: The Burger You've Been Looking For

By Mike Longley · March 16, 2026

Ask around about the best burgers Branford CT has to offer, and you'll keep hearing the same name. Our burgers are hand-formed daily using fresh, never-frozen beef, and served on toasted brioche buns with your choice of toppings. It's the reason locals keep coming back.

Build Your Own Burger at Longley's

Pick your patty, pick your cheese, pick your toppings — keep it simple or stack it high with sautéed mushrooms, a fried onion ring, a fried egg, or gorgonzola.
