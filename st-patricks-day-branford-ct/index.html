Celebrate St. Patrick's Day in Branford, CT at Longley's Restaurant

By Mike Longley · March 9, 2026

When St. Patrick's Day rolls around, there's no better place to celebrate than your neighborhood spot. Longley's — a family-owned casual dining restaurant and bar at 249 West Main Street in Branford — goes all in with live music, special menus, and extended hours.

St. Patrick's Day Food Specials

The kitchen serves up the Irish classics: corned beef and cabbage, shepherd's pie, Irish soda bread, and Reuben sandwiches — all made from scratch. Pair them with a pint of Guinness or an Irish coffee for the full experience.

Drinks, Live Music, and Good Times

Live entertainment, craft cocktails, green beer specials, and Irish whiskey flights — with the bar open late: 1 AM on weekdays and 2 AM on weekends.

Why Celebrate St. Patrick's Day in Branford CT?

Skip the drive to the big city. Branford's charming shoreline community has everything you need for a proper celebration, minus the parking headache.

The Best Spot for St. Paddy's in Branford

Since opening in 2024, Longley's has offered the full package: homemade Irish food specials, a full bar with craft cocktails and draft beer, and live entertainment all in one place.

More Than Just St. Paddy's Day

Any time of year, we've got private parties, a full catering menu, and late night dining — come see us whenever the mood strikes.
