Friday Happy Hour in Branford, CT: Drinks, Apps, and Good Vibes at Longley's

By Mike Longley · March 23, 2026

Looking for the best happy hour Branford CT has to offer? Every Friday from 3 to 6 PM, Longley's rolls out drink specials and appetizer deals that make it the perfect way to kick off your weekend.

What to Expect at Happy Hour

Discounted draft beers, house wines, and well drinks, plus appetizer deals on favorites like crispy wings, loaded nachos, pulled pork sliders, and mozzarella sticks. Our bartenders also mix up featured cocktails that change weekly, so there's always a reason to come back.

The Bar at Longley's

Our bar isn't just a place to grab a drink — it's the heart of the restaurant. Plenty of seating, TVs for the game, and bartenders who'll remember your order by your second visit.

Stay for Dinner

When happy hour wraps up, the night's just getting started. The full dinner menu — burgers, salads, daily specials — runs late, with the kitchen open until 2 AM on Fridays and Saturdays, and live music on the weekends.

Private Events and Group Reservations

Longley's is a great spot for group events any night of the week. We offer private party packages with custom menus and full bar service.

Where to Find the Best Happy Hour in Branford CT

249 West Main Street, Branford, with plenty of parking. Happy hour runs every Friday from 3 to 6 PM — no reservations needed, just walk in.
