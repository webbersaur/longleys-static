More Than Just a Restaurant

Longley's is a top-rated restaurant in Branford CT serving real homemade comfort food since 2023. This family-owned casual dining spot on West Main Street offers a full American menu for lunch, dinner, and late night dining in Branford — with our kitchen open until 1–2 AM.

Our menu features hearty burgers, fresh salads, hand-breaded appetizers, and daily specials — all made from scratch. Pair your meal with a cold draft beer, craft cocktail, or glass of wine from our full bar. Join us every Friday for happy hour from 3 to 6, or check out our live music events on the weekends.

Located near the Branford and East Haven town line, Longley's is the restaurant in Branford CT you can count on for catering and private parties. Stop by, grab a seat at the bar, and discover why locals keep coming back.
