More Than Just a Restaurant

Longley's is a family-owned spot serving up real homemade comfort food since 2023. Whether you're catching the game with friends, grabbing lunch on the patio, or celebrating with the whole crew — we've got you covered.

Our full American menu runs from burgers and salads to hand-breaded appetizers, daily specials, and all the bar-food favorites done right. Pair it with something from our full bar — draft beer, craft cocktails, or an ice-cold pitcher — and settle in.

Located at 249 W Main St, Unit 8 — right in the heart of Branford.
