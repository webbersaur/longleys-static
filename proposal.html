Your current site has done its job — it put Longley's online, drives phone calls, and shows up in local search. It runs on WordPress, which was the right call when it was built. But things have changed, and a simpler, faster site can do even more for you.

Room to Grow

Too many moving parts
WordPress needs plugins, updates, and a database just to show your menu and hours. That's a lot of machinery for a straightforward site.
Speed has a ceiling
Your site uses tools to speed things up, but they're working around the problem. A simpler site is just fast from the start.
You're paying for flexibility you don't use
WordPress is built for site owners who want to log in and edit content themselves. Since we handle all your updates for you, that flexibility just adds extra weight and complexity without giving you anything back.
AI search is the future
People are using ChatGPT, Siri, and Google AI to find restaurants. A clean, simple site is much easier for these tools to read and recommend.
