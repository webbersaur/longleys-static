Why Locals Love Longley's: Branford's Newest Neighborhood Restaurant

By Mike Longley · January 30, 2026

Since opening in 2024, Longley's has quickly become the casual dining spot Branford locals count on. Here's what keeps the neighborhood coming back.

Homemade Food, Every Single Day

Everything here is made fresh daily - homemade soups, hand-formed burgers, hand-breaded appetizers, and rotating specials straight from the kitchen.

What Sets Us Apart from Other Restaurants in Branford CT

Two things: freshness and hours. Food is prepped fresh every day, and our kitchen stays open until 1–2 AM - long after most restaurants in the area have gone dark. Craving something at midnight? Check out our full late night menu.

More Than Just Dinner

Stop in for lunch specials Tuesday through Friday, book one of our private party spaces for your next celebration, or let us bring the food to you with off-premise catering.

Happy Hour and Live Entertainment

Every Friday from 3 to 6 PM, happy hour brings drink specials and good company. On the weekends, we turn up the volume with live entertainment from local performers.

A Real Neighborhood Spot

At the end of the day, Longley's is about treating every guest like family. Grab a seat at the bar, say hi, and you'll see why locals keep coming back.
