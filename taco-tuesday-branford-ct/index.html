Taco Tuesday in Branford, CT: The Best Tacos and Deals at Longley's

By Mike Longley · April 20, 2026

Tuesday nights in Branford belong to tacos. Every week, Longley's rolls out fresh-made tacos and drink specials that have turned Taco Tuesday into a neighborhood tradition.

What's on the Taco Tuesday Menu?

The lineup rotates weekly, from ground beef and seasoned chicken to blackened fish and pulled pork, all made fresh in our kitchen. Top them with house-made pico de gallo, shredded lettuce, sour cream, jalapeños, and our signature sauces, with sides like chips and guacamole, Mexican rice, or black beans.

Drink Specials to Match

The bar joins in with specials on margaritas, Mexican beers, and tequila shots, plus featured cocktails built to pair with the taco menu.

A Tuesday Night Tradition

Families, coworkers, date nights — Taco Tuesday draws a little bit of everyone. And since the kitchen's open until 1 AM on Tuesdays, there's no rush.

More Than Just Tacos

Not feeling tacos? The full menu is always available — including our hand-formed burgers and everything else that makes Longley's Branford's favorite casual dining restaurant.

Join Us for Taco Tuesday

Specials start at 4 PM every Tuesday at 249 West Main Street, Branford. No reservations needed — just walk in.
