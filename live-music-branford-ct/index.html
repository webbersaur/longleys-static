Live Music in Branford, CT: Weekend Entertainment at Longley's

By Mike Longley · March 30, 2026

If you're looking for live music Branford CT locals actually get excited about, Longley's Restaurant is the spot. Talented local and regional performers take the stage on weekends, backed by a full bar, housemade food, and a kitchen that stays open late.

Who Plays at Longley's?

We book a mix of local and regional acts covering everything from classic rock and blues to acoustic sets and party bands. Upcoming lineups are announced on our Facebook page - and right on our homepage.

No Cover, No Hassle

There's no cover charge. Just walk in, grab a seat at the bar or a table, order some food and drinks, and enjoy the show. Weekend nights fill up fast, so come early for the best seats.

Dinner and a Show

Make a night of it. Our kitchen stays open until 2 AM on Fridays and Saturdays, so whether it's our famous burgers before the first set or wings between songs, you can eat whenever the mood strikes.

The Perfect Spot for a Night Out

Date night, birthday, or just a Friday that deserves better - Longley's has become one of the go-to spots for live music Branford CT has to offer.
